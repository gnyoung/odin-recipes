Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

Description

This recipe is for the world's most delicious mac and cheese! If you want your partner to forgive you for being a jerk then make this mac and cheese. Bring this to your next family gathering and you will instantly become the new favorite child. This recipe contains SIX different types of cheeses. It will literally make you drool, so I recommend keeping a stack of napkins nearby.

Ingredients

  • 1lb dried elbow pasta
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/4 cup provolone cheese
  • 3/4 cup muenster cheese
  • 3/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup mild cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup monterey jack cheese
  • 3/4 cup Velveeta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan add 5 cups of water, 1 teaspoon of salt and 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  2. Bring the water to a boil and add the elbow pasta.
  3. Allow the pasta to cook for 7 minutes, stirring occasionally. After 7 minutes, remove the pasta from the stove and drain the water.
  4. Transfer the pasta to a baking pan. Add the melted butter and mix well. Next add the evaporated milk. Add the eggs and mix well. Lastly, add the provolone, muenster, Monterey jack, velveeta, sharp and mild cheddar cheeses and mix well.
  5. Transfer to the preheated oven and bake for 15 minutes.
  6. After 15 minutes stir the mac and cheese then return it to the oven and allow it to bake for an additional 25 minutes.
